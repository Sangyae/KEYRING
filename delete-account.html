Data Deletion Request

At Tiny Crafts, we respect your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us with your request.

Important: Account deletion is permanent. Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered, along with all associated data including:
  • Account profile information
  • Order history
  • Wishlist items
  • Shopping cart

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and data, please email us at:

contact@tinycrafts.com

In your email, please include:

  • Your registered email address
  • A confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account
  • Any additional comments

Processing Time

We will process your deletion request within 30 days. Once processed, all your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems.

Grace Period

If you change your mind, you have 7 days from submission to cancel the deletion request. After 7 days, the deletion process becomes irreversible.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, please contact us at: contact@tinycrafts.com
