The Magic Behind the Fluff 🧶

From a fluffy cloud of wool to a tiny companion on your keys—this is how Tiny Crafts come to life.

Meet the Makers

Tiny Crafts isn't a giant factory; we are a close-knit family. We partner directly with a cooperative of 25 incredibly skilled artisans. By working closely with these makers, we ensure they receive fair, living wages and craft in safe, joyful environments.

Every single stitch, poke, and detail is crafted by human hands. It takes years of practice to master the art of shaping wool, and our team pours their heart into every single character.

The Needle-Felting Journey

  1. Cruelty-Free Sourcing: We start with 100% natural, cruelty-free wool. It is soft, sustainable, and completely biodegradable.
  2. Eco-Dyeing: The wool is dyed using vibrant, non-toxic colors to ensure our pandas are perfectly black and white, and our avocados are perfectly green.
  3. The Magic of the Needle: Using a specialized barbed needle, our artisans repeatedly poke the fluffy wool. The barbs catch the fibers, tangling them together until they slowly sculpt into a solid, adorable shape. It takes thousands of pokes to make just one craft!

From Our Hands to Your Keys

Once a keyring is finished, it undergoes a careful quality check to ensure it is durable enough for daily bumping on bags and keys. Finally, it is wrapped in 100% plastic-free, recyclable packaging and shipped off to its forever home—ready to bring a tiny pop of joy to your day!
